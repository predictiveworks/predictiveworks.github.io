Boost Time to Insight with Plug and Play Analytics

PredictiveWorks. covers all dimensions of Gartner’s Analytic continuum and is an all-in-one platform for Unified Analytics, sitting on the on the shoulders of Apache Spark and Google CDAP.

Apache Spark is the big data platform of choice for enterprises and Google CDAP comes with a unified pipeline technology (at the heart of Google’s Cloud Data Fusion service).

Integrating these open-source giants into a single platform opens the door to visual and code-free data processing, thereby unifying

  • data integration,
  • deep learning,
  • machine learning,
  • business rule execution,
  • query processing,
  • time series analysis and
  • natural language processing.

The Result

World’s largest collection of code-free and self-serve data pipeline plugins

PredictiveWorks. minimizes both time-to-insight and required skills, and defines a new era of business-ready data analytics.
