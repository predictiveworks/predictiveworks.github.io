Binarizer

The Binarizer transforms numerical feature vectors to binary (0 or 1) vectors by applying a value threshold. Values greater than the threshold are binarized to 1.0. Values equal to or less than the threshold are binarized to 0.0.

@Plugin(type = SparkCompute.PLUGIN_TYPE)
@Name("Binarizer")
@Description("A transformation stage that leverages the Apache Spark ML Binarizer "
    + "to map continuous features onto binary values.")
