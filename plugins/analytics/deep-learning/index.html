Works DL

The foundation of PredictiveWorks. deep learning support is Intel's BigDL library. The scope of this library was standardized as plugins for Google CDAP data pipelines.

The current version of Works DL is actually part of the commercial portfolio of Dr. Krusche & Partner. It is planned to open-source this library until mids of 2020.
